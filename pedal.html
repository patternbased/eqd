OnomatoPedal

OnomatoPedal is an audio playground and a series of downloadable sample packs and music tracks inspired by the Japanese Onomotopoetic words and the music effects pedals of the world famous EarthQuaker Devices.

How To Play

The main screen will display several EarthQuaker pedals. Click on 'Shuffle Pedals' to get a new set of choices. Once you see a pedal/onomotopeia that you would like to explore, click on it. On the desktop version, you will be able to play a set of sounds with the following 12 keys on your computer keyboard: z,x,c,v,a,s,d,f,q,w,e,r. On a mobile device, click to enter 'Play Mode', the track will start and you will see twelve drum pads. You can play along with the track or pause the track to just play the pads. On the desktop version, you will also see an option to download the sample pack files. Each pedal also has a link to it's official page at the EarthQuaker website.
